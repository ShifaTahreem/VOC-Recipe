Chocolate Cake

⏱ Prep Time: 45 mins
🍽 Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Whisk eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; combine with dry mix.
  4. Pour into pans and bake for 25–30 minutes (check doneness).
  5. Let cool, then frost as desired.
